DELIGHTS SPECIAL



Indian Style Vegetable Curry

Our hotel’s signature Indian-style vegetable curry features fresh seasonal vegetables 
    in a rich, spiced tomato and coconut sauce, perfectly balanced with fragrant 
        cumin and our special garam masala. Served with basmati rice or naan, 
                                        it's an authentic taste of Indian tradition.

Paneer Tikka Masala

  Our hotel’s signature Paneer Tikka Masala showcases tender, marinated paneer cubes cooked 
                in a rich, spiced tomato and cream sauce, infused with our chef’s special 
                            blend of aromatic spices. Paired with basmati rice or naan, 
                                it’s a perfect fusion of creamy, tangy and smoky flavors.

Crepes with pineapple and coconut

Start your day the tropical way with these deliciously simple coconut and pineapple 
 crepes. Delicate crepes envelop juicy pineapple and a sprinkle of toasted coconut, 
   creating a perfect harmony of tropical sweetness and light, nutty crunch. Finished 
          with a drizzle of coconut cream, each bite is a blissful escape to the islands.

Tofu Noodle soup

Our hotel’s signature Tofu Noodle Soup features soft tofu and fresh vegetables in a light, savory 
            broth, enhanced with delicate herbs and spices. Served with tender noodles, it offers 
                        a comforting blend of warmth and wholesome flavors in every bowl.




OUR CHEFS

Anahita Dhonde

Anahita Dhondy is the Chef Manager at the famous 
Bombay Irani Cafe and Bar, SodabottleOpenerWala. 
Her career went to another level as she introduced 
the best of the Parsi cuisine through SodaBottle-
OpenerWala creating a whole new trend around the 
best restaurant chains in the country. She also won 
‘The Young Chef’ award, Times Food Award and the 
Hospitality Sector ‘Indian of the year’ title.

Bani Nanda

Bani Nanda is a well-known Indian pastry chef and 
the founder of Miam Patisserie, a boutique bakery 
in New Delhi, India. She gained recognition for her 
creative, high-quality desserts and modern French 
pastry techniques.

Radhika Khandelwal

Radhika Khandelwal is a prominent Indian chef and 
the owner of Fig & Maple, a well-regarded farm-to-table 
restaurant in New Delhi. She is known for her emphasis 
on sustainability, zero-waste cooking, and using locally 
sourced ingredients. Radhika’s culinary philosophy revolves 
around minimizing food waste, supporting local farmers, and 
creating innovative dishes using seasonal produce.
